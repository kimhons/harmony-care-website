### Critical Gaps Identified:

#### Gap 1: **Specific Pain Point Deep-Dives**

Your existing articles cover AI broadly, but lack deep dives into specific operational challenges that keep administrators up at night. You mention medication management, family communication, and staffing in passing, but don't dedicate full articles to these critical pain points.

**Why this matters:** Decision-makers search for solutions to specific problems, not general AI information. "medication error prevention software" gets more qualified traffic than "AI in healthcare."

#### Gap 2: **Social Proof & Credibility**

You have ROI numbers but no named case studies or customer success stories. Prospects in the decision stage need to see themselves in your customers' shoes.

**Why this matters:** B2B buyers need proof that solutions work for facilities like theirs. Generic success metrics are less persuasive than "Here's how Pine Grove Manor reduced medication errors by 73%."

#### Gap 3: **Competitive Differentiation**

Your articles explain why AI matters but don't address "Why HarmonyCare vs. other solutions?" Prospects comparing vendors need content that highlights your unique approach.

**Why this matters:** Late-stage buyers are comparing 2-3 vendors. Without differentiation content, you're competing solely on price.
