## Campaign Goals
1. **Engagement**: Keep founding members excited and informed
2. **Retention**: Prevent signup abandonment
3. **Education**: Build product knowledge and confidence
4. **Community**: Foster founding member community
5. **Conversion**: Ensure smooth transition to paid customers at launch

## Success Metrics
- Email open rates (target: >40%)
- Click-through rates (target: >15%)
- Beta program participation (target: >30% of signups)
- Launch conversion rate (target: >80% of founding members)
