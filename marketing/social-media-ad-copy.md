## A/B Testing Strategy

### Test Variables:

1. **Hook Type:** Fear vs. Aspiration vs. Data-driven
2. **CTA:** "Reserve Spot" vs. "Calculate Savings" vs. "Get Assessment"
3. **Visual:** Staff burnout vs. Happy residents vs. Dashboard screenshot
4. **Offer:** Founding member discount vs. ROI calculator vs. Free assessment
5. **Length:** Short (100 words) vs. Medium (200 words) vs. Long (300+ words)

### Success Metrics:

- Click-through rate (CTR): Target 2-4%
- Cost per lead (CPL): Target $15-30
- Landing page conversion: Target 15-25%
- Cost per acquisition (CPA): Target $150-300
